About

Who we are

Ours is a München-based community built around shared experience. Cycling, conversation, and the kind of gatherings where you leave knowing someone better than when you arrived.

No brand partnerships. No content strategy. Just people doing things together, with care.

The backstory

Ours started with a few Saturday morning rides along the Isar. No club, no structure, just a group chat and a meeting point. The routes got longer, the coffee stops got better, and the group grew through word of mouth.

What kept people coming back wasn't the cycling. It was the feeling of being part of something unforced. A room where nobody was performing. So we started asking: what else could work like that?

That question became the Salon — a seasonal series of small gatherings built around a single theme. Different spaces, interesting people, real talk. And it became Stories — a place to write honestly about what it means to live in this city, move through it, and belong to it.

What guides us

Show up as you are

People over content

Ours exists for the people in the room, not the people watching from outside. No cameras pushed in faces, no manufactured moments. If something's worth photographing, someone will photograph it. That's different from designing the experience around the photo.

Two languages, one group

English and German, freely mixed. München is an international city and Ours reflects that. If you can communicate, you belong here.

Intentional, not exclusive

Ours is open to anyone who shows up with good energy. There's no membership, no vetting. But there is an expectation: be present, be respectful, bring something to the table even if that something is just curiosity.

Built in the open

Ours is a work in progress and that's the point. The format will evolve, the programming will shift, new ideas will come from the community itself. The only thing that stays fixed is the commitment to doing things with care.
