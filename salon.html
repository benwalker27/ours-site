Salon

Season One

Beyond the bike, Ours runs a seasonal series of gatherings. Evenings in different spaces around München, each exploring a different theme. Part salon, part dinner party, part something that doesn't have a name yet.

Small rooms. Interesting people. Real conversation.

01
The Room
An introduction
An evening of music and conversation. An introduction to Ours, some records, and seeing who turns up. The first gathering. No agenda beyond being present.
02
Borrowed Confidence
On authenticity when AI makes everyone sound smarter
When you use AI to write something better than you'd write naturally, who is actually communicating? A provocation and small-group conversation about what's real.
03
Slow
On pace, attention, and what we lose when everything is optimised
A hands-on evening in a workshop space. Make something with your hands. Talk about what it means to do things at the speed they actually deserve.
04
Strangers
On building real connection in a transient city
A long table, a good meal, and people you don't know yet. Structured conversation over dinner. Not networking, just honest exchange between interesting people.
05
Home
On belonging somewhere you weren't born
Short personal stories from people in the community about home, identity, and what it means to build a life in a place that isn't where you started.
06
Outside
Season finale
Back on bikes. A long day ride with the people you've spent a winter getting to know in rooms. The ride is the punctuation mark.
