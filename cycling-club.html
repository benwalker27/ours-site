On the bike

Ride guidelines

Group riding has its own etiquette. These are the basics for Ours CC rides, whether it's your first group ride or your thousandth.

Together first

Every ride has a ride-together pace. If you want to send it, there's space for that. But the group stays connected. Regroup at the top, regroup after turns, nobody gets left behind unless they choose to push ahead.

Well led

Clear road calls, predictable group behaviour, routes recce'd before you ride them. New to group riding? You'll learn how it works fast. Experienced? You'll appreciate that everyone else knows too.

Road rules

Ride two abreast maximum. Single out when traffic needs space. Point out hazards for the rider behind you. No half-wheeling. If you don't know what any of that means, that's fine. You will after one Saturday.

Bring what you need

A spare tube, a pump or CO2, and enough food and water for the distance. If you're not sure what to bring, ask in the group chat before the ride. Nobody minds the question.
