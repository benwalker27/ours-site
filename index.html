Ours

München

Ours was born out of a craving for authentic, unfiltered experiences, indoors and out. The kind where you show up as yourself, meet people worth knowing, and leave realising you haven't checked your phone in hours. It's a work in progress, so come be a part of it!

We're starting with cycling. Riding bikes takes us beautiful places, away from the noise of everyday life, and often opens up room for real talks. Whether weekday mornings through the Isartal, long days into the foothills, or weekends further afield, join us to stretch your legs and escape your screens.

Ours should be more than just cycling though. It's a commitment to shared experience, done with intention, open to everyone who's willing to show up as themselves and play nice with others. We'll explore new formats as we grow, so watch this space for discussion sessions, record pull parties, and salon style themed evenings.

English and German spoken. No assholes tolerated.

How things work

Show up as you are

Conversations over content

Ours exists for the people in the room, not the people watching from outside. If you feel the need to document, ask permission, and keep it authentic. This isn't the place to show up with your Meta glasses and a GoPro.

Two languages, one group

English and German, freely mixed. München is an international city and Ours reflects that. If you can communicate, you belong here.

Intentional, not exclusive

Ours is open to anyone who shows up with good energy. There's no membership, no vetting. But there is an expectation: be present, be respectful, bring something to the table even if that something is just curiosity.

Built on the go

Ours is a work in progress and that's the point. The format will evolve, the programming will shift, new ideas will come from the community itself. The only thing that stays fixed is the commitment to doing things with care.

Stay in the loop

Come find us
